Changes made

Voting.html - Instead of having thumbnails for each anime in the voting list, we decided to use only text (the name of the anime and its year of release). We made this decision because this list can be quite long - in our example, there are 12 options to rank. This is a representative number, judging from voting rounds performed in real-life scenarious prior semesters. Thumbnails usually require large amounts of space on the screen to be recognizable. In order to keep the list managable and to keep it easy to drag and drop, we decided not to use thumbnails.

We also decided to deviate slightly from our suggested color scheme. Instead of having the submit button dark like in the mockup (figure 12 of P2), we instead opted for a brighter color, so as to have the button be easily visible. Finally, we chose to omit the white arrows on the left-hand side of each list item, because in the end we deemed this to be a distraction. Instead, we added textual instruction on the top of the page.
