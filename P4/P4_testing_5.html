Tester Audience

For testing, we performed both surveying with a web form, and live user testing. A Google Form was sent to members of Anime NTNU and other groups of interest. For user testing, the group members contacted friends and acquaintances, gave them some questions and tasks, and observed their responses. In total, 13 responses from the webform were submitted, and 8 live tests were performed.

Considering that Anime NTNU is a student organization, the target audience consists mainly of students. This has been reflected in our testing procedure, considering that all the people participating in live testing and the majority of webform submitters were students.

Testing Platforms

We were able to run tests with a variety of platforms and web browsers. The platforms covered were Windows 10, Mac, and mobile. Among the web browsers used were Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Firefox, Safari, and Vivaldi.

Observed testing

In addition to the digital schema we sent out, we conducted eight live tests where we observed how a test person interacted with the website. The test person chose their unit and browser of preference and tested different features and functionalities available on the website. As we live tested quite a lot of people, we would like to highlight the testing procedure and feedback received from two of the testers. At the end of this section, we will briefly state the overall issues, opinions and feedback received from all the live tests.

The tasks performed in the live testing were the following:

  1. How can you contact Anime NTNU?
  2. Find information about the first upcoming screening.
  3. What anime are being watched this semester?
  4. When is the last screening of the semester?
  5. What anime were being watched in the fall of 2013?
  6. Vote for which anime you would like to watch.
  7. What is Sayonara Zetsubou Sensei about?
  8. What is Anime NTNU?
  9. Create a user.
  10. How can you attend screenings?

Test person using a Windows desktop computer with Google Chrome

This person was able to navigate to the desired elements very quickly. They used the navigation bar extensively. They immediately understand how to arrange the elements in the voting list.

They explained that they found it easy to find information, they found the voting page practical, and they thought it was positive that information about all upcoming screenings was published. However, they found it impractical that they had to join the Discord server to find more information. They were uncertain in which order the episodes were shown. They also wanted the ability to show voting results.
